Original Website

The Ladd Observatory

The Ladd Observatory website is rather outdated. Old photos, poorly sized text, and huge margins plague the website with uninviting features that appear bare at an initial glance.

Design Flaws:

  • Small text, not uniformly distributed
  • Outdated, limited photos
  • Content does not take up the entire screen
  • Very large borders of blank space
  • Bare homepage, yet numerous amount of linked pages
  • Limitedly interactive

Accesibility:

 WebAIM WAVE detected that text on the Ladd Observatory website was too small and posed readability issues. I agree and believe that not only the size of the text, but the space between texts makes it hard for users to read through. Alerts were also made about the poor sizing of the main content area and personally, the unnecessarily large borders were the first thing I noticed when visiting the page.
